Who We Are

Homes that feel global in finish and local in soul.

Assas Designs was built on one simple idea: African homes can be deeply contemporary without losing the warmth, shade, texture, and social feeling that make them belong to their place.

We design for sun, airflow, privacy, family gatherings, and long-term comfort. That means cleaner forms, softer palettes, smarter orientation, and spaces that still feel lived in and welcoming.

How We Work

A process built around place, comfort, and clarity

01

Listen and map

We start with lifestyle, family patterns, budget, and how you want the home to feel every day.

02

Read the site

Sun paths, breeze, privacy, rain, and neighborhood context shape the early design decisions.

03

Shape the home

We build a concept around courtyards, room flow, material warmth, and indoor-outdoor balance.

04

Refine the details

Joinery, lighting, openings, finishes, and shade devices are tuned so the house stays cohesive.

05

Guide the build

We stay close through the final delivery and remain your partner for future refinements.
